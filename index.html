Automated Garden Management System

I've developed a device for monitoring environmental conditions like temperature and humidity, with the ability to toggle functions via a potentiometer knob. All data is displayed on a 3.5'' LCD screen. My future plans involve gathering additional data such as soil humidity, pH levels, and solar radiation. With this data, I aim to identify trends and patterns. Furthermore, I intend to create an automated watering system that activates when the soil is dry. Ultimately, I aspire to scale this system for larger gardening operations.
